About Us Page

A new chapter in the Sri Lankan Fitness Industry started as Rota Fitness opened its doors to the public. Rota Fitness aims to inspire active living, and give you access to credible health and fitness resources and experiences all in one comprehensive platform. We collaborate with professionals across the network to curate experiences, corporate seminars, and inspirational content as well.

This site provides you the most effective workout packages according to your requirement and this is the best virtual training platform in Sri Lanka. Explore the site for activities and events that may interest you, search for classes, new experiences, find healthy diets and much more!

Our Team

Amaya Karunarathne

CEO & Trainer

Amaya Karunarathne has spent the past two decades in the health and fitness industry. Her passion for fitness is the platform she has built her life on. Through her dedication to unlocking the power of possibility that lies within us all, Amaya guides her students to uncover the best within themselves. Amaya competed at the highest level of her chosen sport, Aerobic Gymnastics, winning three gold medals and placing 8th in the world in 2009. Amaya's life story is one of success. She has triumphed over obstacles and has not let life circumstances define her. She is a living testament that the power of will and belief can take you to the Nth degree. Amaya is the founder of Rota Fitness Training Center in the heart of colombo, Sri Lanka. Rota Fitness caters to a diverse client base ranging from professional athletes to cardio rehab patients. Amaya is committed to meeting her students where they are and inviting them to find their new edge of possibility. Her mission and message resonate profoundly across the board–Amaya doesn't just "train" people, she strives to inspire them to reach higher and dig deeper for themselves. Amaya is a Yoga Alliance Continuing Education Provider (YACEP), which recognizes her as an expert in one or more of yoga alliance’s continuing education categories. With hundreds of hours and counting of yoga teacher training, in all of Amaya’s classes clients can expect a powerful flow and creative sequencing that’s accessible to all levels and designed to empower the student.
